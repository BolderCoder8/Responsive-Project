A Little About Me...

Originally from Ireland, I’ve called sunny Sydney my home for over 8 years. With over 10 years experience as a corporate Executive Assistant for Senior Management in Ireland and Australia, I decided to focus my time to assist small business owners grow and maintain their businesses in April 2020. I’m passionate about organisation and streamlining processes. I’ve worked with some great CEO’s and senior management in companies such as Lufthansa, McCann Australia, Mott MacDonald, Thomas Hamel and The Star Casino Sydney. I’m a little OCD (but in a good way). I’m ready to help you get on top of your admin workload. I know what it takes to implement processes and strategies and the benefits of placing systems in any size business. So let me take care of all over the background tasks, while you focus on the front and centre of your business.
